About the LG G2

Introducing the award-winning LG G2

Equipped with the latest cutting edge technology, the award winning LG G2 is a true powerhouse smartphone that delivers greater speed in a sophisticated slim line design. Its full HD display coupled with a near zero bezel screen and huge battery 3000mAh battery mean that you can consumer media better and longer than ever before.

Available in both black and white.

Key Features

  • 5.2" Full HD IPS display with near zero bezel
  • 13 MP OIS Camera
  • Long lasting 3000mAH battery
  • Instinctive design with refined rear key
  • Knock ON – the new way to turn your device on and off

All about Knockon...

A simple knocking gesture is all it takes. Just knock twice on the G2 to gently wake your phone’s display without picking it up or pressing the power key. G2 senses the gesture and turns the display on.

Amazing Battery Life

The G2 features a large 3000mAh capacity battery with SiO+ technology. This best in class battery and processor offers two times better power consumption, so you can enjoy all the multi-tasking you need and want for longer.

13-megapixel OIS camera

The stunning 13-megapixel camera delivers crystal clear photos even under dark or shaky conditions with the built-in Optical Image Stabiliser. It's great for night photography too - lower light settings usually need longer exposures, but with OIS you can get amazing low-light shots. Plus, Multi-point Auto Focus (AF) initiates fast and accurate focus for multiple faces or moving objects.
